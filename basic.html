Sono is a Town in Sono Block in Jamui District of Bihar State, India. It belongs to Munger Division . It is located 27 KM towards South from District head quarters Jamui. It is a Block head quarter. Sono Pin code is 811314 and postal head office is Sono . Sono is surrounded by Jhajha Block towards North , Khaira Block towards North , Gidhor Block towards North , Chakai Block towards East . Jhajha , Jamui , Deoghar , Kharagpur are the nearby Cities to Sono.
